Gear

There is a lot of gear options in Due Process, and each one serves a different purpose. Here you will find a long list of each gear item you can use in the game, and a short description about it.

Attacker Weapons

Attacker weapons are used by the ARGUS cops whenever they are trying to infiltrate the building.

Weapon Range Description Image
AP-25 Mid The AP-25 is a fully automatic firearm based off of the real life Vector SMG. No image available.
BLK-TAR Long The BLK-TAR is the long(er)-range semi-automatic rifle of the attackers. It has 30 round STANAG mags in a bullpup style.
DL-12 Short The DL-12 is the only shotgun available to attackers. It is pump-action, and holds a decent amount of ammo.
PK-57 Short The DL-12 is the only shotgun available to attackers. It is pump-action, and holds a decent amount of ammo. No image available.
SAB-R Long The DL-12 is the only shotgun available to attackers. It is pump-action, and holds a decent amount of ammo. No image available.

Defender Weapons

Defender weapons are used by the crims (criminals) whenever they are trying to prevent ARGUS from entering the building and defusing the bomb.

Weapon Range Description Image
KR-82U Short / Mid The KR-82U is a fully automatic firearm. The weapon's main traits are the large thirty round magazine and high damage per bullet, along with having good bullet penetration.
KR-82 Long The KR-82 features a large thirty round magazine and high damage per bullet, along with having low overall recoil due to the full size and inclusion of a stock. This weapon has been removed from the game.
F1-Legros Mid / Long The F1-Legros, usually shortened to just "Legros" is a long barreled semi-automatic rifle designed for long range engagement.
INGMAR-57 Mid / Long It has a twenty round magazine with high damage capacity and a medium rate of fire, capable of two-shot headshotting any opponent if the recoil is controlled properly.
Gat-9 Short The Gat-9, usually shortened to "gat" is a 9mm handheld pistol which has a high fire rate, decent size mag but overall low damage.
LS45 Short The LS45 is a powerful semi automatic pistol with a limited magazine size, taking up the secondary slot and available exclusively to Defenders.
Super Shorty Short Holding only three shots in the magazine including the one chamber, this gun is a hold-out weapon at best, and even then it's range is very poor.
Gruber-5 Short / Mid eaturing a 30 round mag and decent fire rate, along with low damage, the gun is the typical SMG style gun.
NACK-11 Short The Nack-11, usually just shortened to "NACK" is an extreme fire rate, extreme recoil SMG. It's recoil is so high that it's nearly uncontrollable in aim down sight mode.
TUB-12 Short It has a decently quick reload speed which can be interrupted per shell, allowing defenders to efficiently weave in and out of confrontations. No image available.

Defender Power Weapons

Defender power weapons are used by the crims to do fulfill a role against ARGUS. They are powerful, but are quite dangerous, as the attackers can pick up the weapons and use them against the defenders.

Weapon Range Description Image
Auto Shotgun Short The Auto Shotgun is a large drum fed automatic shotgun capable of rapidly dispatching opponents. It has devastating close range capabilities.
MAWP Long This weapon does a massive amount of damage, enough to kill with a single headshot on any target and a bodyshot on most. Attackers can withstand a body shot, but it leaves them with extremely low health. No image available.
Shopping Cart Technical Mid The DL-12 is the only shotgun available to attackers. It is pump-action, and holds a decent amount of ammo. No image available.
